TODAY I NOTICED I HAVE BEEN ALIVE


... and what if all tears disappear in the rain
and every scream is drowned by the music I make,
no tomorrow ever felt welcome to me
and no kiss did excite me?

I’ve forgotten to feel,
I’ve unlearned how to breath.

Didn’t even come to realize:
In my head I died a long time ago ...

MORE THAN I MEANT TO


... Today I noticed I’ve been alive one year more than I meant
more than I would have spent,
more days to remember,
more days to pretend
that my head holds no chaos
and my heart does not ache,
my skin is not crawling,
I do not depend
on pills that I treasure
like an old friend
’til one day I might say
I don’t want this to end.

All I fear - more.
All I want - more.
